## Medium Description (Full App Store / Website)

**"Steady is a personal health tracking app designed for people with Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome (EDS) and dysautonomia, especially POTS. 

Track what matters: guided POTS stand tests, symptom & trigger logs, daily mood check-ins, and sleep/heart rate trends from your Fitbit exports. Steady automatically spots patterns — compare good vs. rough days to understand what makes your condition worse.

Everything stays on your phone. Zero network calls. No cloud sync, no accounts, no ads. Your health data is yours alone.

Features:
✓ POTS stand test (guided, with HR threshold alerts)
✓ Symptom & trigger logging (quick tap-to-log)
✓ Daily mood tracking
✓ Trends & pattern insights
✓ Import Fitbit/Google Health data (optional)
✓ Backup & restore
✓ 100% offline

Open-source and free."**
